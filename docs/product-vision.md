# Product Vision

## Product summary

Delirium Buddy is a local-first mobile app that helps carers and family members record daily changes that may be useful in care conversations.

The app tracks observable changes such as confusion, agitation, sleep, fever or infection, medication changes, hallucination, falls, hydration, eating, pain, mobility, and sudden changes from usual behaviour.

It provides:

- A simple daily check-in
- A person profile / baseline
- Risk-style personal tracking signal summaries
- 7-day shareable summaries
- History and entry review

## Intended MVP users

The first MVP is for:

- Family carers
- Informal carers
- Patients or loved ones tracking changes privately
- People preparing notes for GP, nurse, hospital, or aged-care conversations

## Current positioning

Delirium Buddy is not a diagnostic tool. It is a personal tracking and communication-support app.

Safe language:

- Personal tracking
- Care conversation support
- Shareable summary
- Observable changes
- Red flags noted
- Baseline comparison

Avoided language:

- Diagnoses delirium
- Predicts delirium
- Detects delirium medically
- Prevents delirium
- Replaces clinical assessment
- AI doctor / clinical decision engine

## Long-term product direction

The long-term direction is a multi-layer product:

1. Consumer/carer mobile app
2. Clinic or aged-care staff mode
3. Secure backend sync
4. Tenant-based organisation management
5. Role-based access control
6. Care team dashboard
7. Automation and reminders
8. Safe AI-assisted summaries, reviewed by humans

## Market opportunity

Many delirium-related tools focus on assessment or education only. Delirium Buddy aims to become more useful by combining:

- Carer observation
- Baseline tracking
- Red flag capture
- Shareable summaries
- Future care workflow support

The immediate opportunity is to create a polished, trustworthy, local-first app that helps carers communicate changes clearly.
