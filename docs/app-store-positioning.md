# Delirium Buddy App Store Safe Positioning

This document provides safe, release-ready positioning language for Delirium Buddy.

## Short description

Delirium Buddy helps carers track daily changes, support structured check-ins, set personal reminders and prepare clear handovers for care conversations.

## Longer description

Delirium Buddy is a local-first care support app for recording daily observations, comparing changes with a person’s usual baseline, reviewing recent trends and preparing concise handover summaries.

It is designed to help family carers and care supporters keep clearer notes for conversations with health and care professionals.

The app can support:

- daily check-ins
- baseline comparison
- structured screening support
- personal reminders
- recent trend review
- handover preparation
- local data export chosen by the user

## Safety wording

Use this wording in store metadata, onboarding and public materials:

> Delirium Buddy is a personal tracking and care conversation support tool. It is not a diagnosis tool and does not replace professional assessment.

## Privacy wording

Use this wording when describing storage and sharing:

> Your information stays on this device unless you choose to share or export a handover.

## Reminder wording

Use this wording when describing reminders:

> Reminders are personal prompts only. They do not alert staff or send data outside the app.

## What to avoid saying

Do not say Delirium Buddy:

- diagnoses delirium
- detects delirium automatically
- predicts delirium
- prevents delirium
- treats delirium
- monitors patients clinically
- alerts staff
- contacts emergency services
- replaces medical assessment
- replaces clinical judgement
- provides medical advice

## App store category suggestion

Recommended category direction:

- Health & Fitness or Medical may both require careful declarations depending on store requirements.
- Position the app as carer support, personal tracking and handover preparation, not medical diagnosis or treatment.

## Suggested subtitle options

- Daily tracking for care conversations
- Check-ins, reminders and handovers
- Track changes and prepare handovers

## Suggested keywords

Use carefully and honestly:

- carer
- care notes
- handover
- daily check-in
- reminder
- baseline
- aged care
- family care
- health notes
- observation

Avoid keyword stuffing or claims that imply diagnosis or treatment.

## Suggested screenshot captions

1. Track daily changes with simple check-ins.
2. Compare observations with the person’s usual baseline.
3. Prepare clear handovers for care conversations.
4. Set personal reminders to repeat observations.
5. Keep information on-device unless you choose to share.

## Public pilot positioning

For aged care or nurse-led pilot conversations:

> Delirium Buddy helps carers capture structured daily observations and prepare clearer handovers. It is designed to support conversations, not replace clinical assessment.

## Release note for first version

> Initial release with daily check-ins, person profile and baseline, structured screening support, reminders, trends, history and handover summaries.
